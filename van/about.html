About Us

Gweneveia Electronics is a leading service brand in global emerging markets, providing service for communications equipment, home appliances, consumer electronics, new energy, IT services and supply chains. The business covers countries in Africa, the Middle East, Eastern Europe, Southeast Asia, South Asia and Latin America. It has more than 2,300 service centers and provides more than 20 million online and offline services to consumers annually.
